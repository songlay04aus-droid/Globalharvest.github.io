Farm Maintenance
Farm Maintenance Worker

Job Description

Responsible for supporting overall farm maintenance tasks including soil preparation, weed removal, irrigation system care, and basic maintenance of agricultural tools and equipment. This position is suitable for people who enjoy varied tasks and have basic mechanical skills.

Responsibilities:

  • Prepare planting areas, till the soil, and remove weeds
  • Assist with repairing farm infrastructure such as fences and water pipe systems
  • Clean and maintain agricultural tools after use
  • Follow safety rules when operating basic machinery
