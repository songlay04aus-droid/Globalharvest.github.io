Harvest Worker
Harvest Worker

Job Description

Responsible for harvesting seasonal agricultural produce such as apples, oranges, grapes, strawberries, and other crops in Australia's leading agricultural areas. This job requires patience and attention to ensure that the products maintain their best quality before being sent to packing facilities.

Responsibilities:

  • Harvest fruits and agricultural products according to correct methods
  • Inspect the initial quality of the produce and discard damaged ones
  • Pack the produce into baskets or containers according to farm standards
  • Able to work outdoors in various weather conditions
