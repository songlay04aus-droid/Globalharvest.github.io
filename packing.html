Packing House Worker
Packing and Sorting Worker

Job Description

Work in the packing shed with the primary responsibility of sorting, grading, and packing agricultural products into boxes for export and distribution. This job requires attention to detail and speed while working with the production line.

Responsibilities:

  • Sort products by size, color, and grade as specified
  • Inspect for defects and discard any substandard produce
  • Carefully pack produce into boxes or packaging to prevent damage
  • Arrange packed boxes on pallets and maintain cleanliness in the work area
